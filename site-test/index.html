We're here to support you

We know college is hard. Especially when you don't live with your parents or people who take care of you. So we want to make it a little easier by providing the essentials that you absolutely need to get through the day. Essentials include things that prevent small disruptions from becoming hour-long problems, basics like hydration, nutrition, and device power, and supplies that protect attention and reduce unnecessary distractions.

Locally sourced supply

Procurement is intentionally simple. If something is truly essential, it must be easy to replace quickly. In practice, that means we walk to Target and buy it for you. No complicated suppliers, no special orders, and no waiting weeks.

If you need something that isn’t listed, submit a request and we’ll review it for practical value and availability.

Living a broke college life?

That's completely fine. Just prepare your essentials kit and leave it in the cart. We won't charge you until you check out, and you can check out at any time — even if it's the middle of a hectic week and you forgot to pack your charger.
